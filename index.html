About Me

Hi! I'm Gabriela, a trilingual Brazilian student at Brigham Young University pursuing a Bachelor's in Pre-Business with a focus on Strategic Management. I previously earned an Associate of Science in Social Media Marketing from Ensign College.

I'm passionate about the intersection of strategy, people, and impact. From coordinating mental health programs at Fiocruz in Rio de Janeiro to representing women-owned brands at Costco roadshows, I thrive where communication meets purpose. I speak English, Portuguese, and Spanish — and believe that understanding people is the foundation of everything.

I'm a member of the Strategy Society, Women in Strategy Association, and Management Consulting Association at BYU, where I'm sharpening my skills in consulting, case interviews, and leadership.
