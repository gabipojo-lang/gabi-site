About Me

I'm Gabriela, a pre-business student at Brigham Young University pursuing a Bachelor of Science with a focus on Strategic Management. Originally from Rio de Janeiro, Brazil, I completed my Associate of Science in Social Media Marketing at Ensign College before transferring to BYU.

My background spans psychology, international services, and direct sales — giving me a lens on business that combines behavioral insight with strategic thinking. I'm actively involved in the Management Consulting Association, Strategy Society, and Women in Strategy Association at BYU.

My goal is a career in corporate strategy or management consulting, where I can help organizations solve complex problems and build competitive advantage.

  • Strategy Society — BYU
  • Women in Strategy Association — BYU
